Girls' Angle Mentors

Mentors are the heart and soul of Girls' Angle.

Mentors Love mathematics, have a deep understanding of mathematics and desire to help foster and nurture girls' interest and ability in mathematics. Mentors take a very individual approach to education. They make every effort to get to know each member as a unique individual and they custom tailor their instruction to each individual's needs and personality.

At every meet, there is at least one mentor present who has proven and published original theorems in mathematics. This enables club members to discover mathematics as a living creative art.

Ultimately, mentors help members improve the way they think using mathematics as an ideal vehicle. Improved ability to think is useful in all facets of life.

Head Mentor

Grace Work is our Head Mentor. She holds a doctoral degree in mathematics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. She arrives from Vanderbilt University where she was Assistant Professor of Mathematics. Ms. Work holds the title of Research Affilate at MIT's Mathematics Department.

Email: gwork "at" girlsangle.org
