Membership

Download the membership application.

A Girls' Angle Membership includes:

  • Email access to our mentors. Members are invited to send us math questions, comments, problem solutions, and content suggestions. Members can expect us to respond constructively. The only thing we won't do is your homework!
  • A 1-year subscription to the print version of the Girls' Angle Bulletin.
  • Discounts on all our offerings.

To join, download the membership application, fill it out, and mail it back to us.
