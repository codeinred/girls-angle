The Girls' Angle Bulletin

The Bulletin is our bimonthly math magazine. It contains articles, interviews, math-inspired art, club notes, problem sets, and more. Content is largely driven by current member and subscriber interests. The Bulletin is designed to instigate further exploration and study of mathematics. When you are able to read through a Bulletin without difficulty, you are well-prepared for any college math major program in the country.

Submissions are welcome.

Women In Mathematics Videos

Conceived by Girls' Angle director Elisenda Grigsby, the Women In Mathematics Video series presents female mathematicians describing some piece of math that excited them when they were students.

If you are a woman in mathematics and would like to contribute to this series, please contact us.
